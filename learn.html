What Is Mail in Voting and Absentee ballots?

Mail-in ballot, Any qualified voter may apply for a mail-in ballot. You may simply request this ballot without a reason. Absentee ballot, If you plan to be out of the municipality on election day or if you have a disability or illness, you should request this ballot type, which still requires you to list a reason for your ballot."

Steps in Registering

There are four steps if you'd like to participate in mail-in voting.

  • First you have to register to vote, request your mail-in ballot, complete your ballot - correctly, and return your ballot.
  • Second, you need to request the mail-in ballot. It's as simple as filling out a form online. Or you can submit the request to your local election office. Do this early as possible. The deadline is October 27, 2020 if you'd like to vote for the presidential election.
  • Third, fill out your ballot carefully and correctly. Two big mistakes voters make is filling out incorrectly and signing it incorrectly.
